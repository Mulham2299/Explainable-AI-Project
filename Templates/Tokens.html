Tokens
Tokens are word pieces; they are basic units of text that a language model can process. Small words may equal one token but longer words like compounds may be multiple, running for example may be broken down into ‘run’ and ‘ning’. Therefore, a smaller max_tokens will create a shorter response, and a higher max_tokens allow the space for a longer response and more detail. This feature is context-dependent. A small value is accurate for concise answers, whereas a higher value is useful for longer detailed responses.
